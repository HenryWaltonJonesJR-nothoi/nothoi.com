Who We Are

Nothoi LLC ("Nothoi," "we," "us") provides private AI assistant and notification services. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including name and contact information (email address, phone number), communication preferences, and information provided through our services or correspondence with us.

How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to deliver our notification and assistant services, send system status alerts, briefings, and task-related messages via SMS, respond to your inquiries, and maintain and improve our services.

SMS/MMS Messaging

If you opt in to receive SMS messages from Nothoi LLC, your phone number and messaging consent records are used solely to deliver the messages you have requested. We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or opt-in data with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Messaging consent is not shared with or sold to any third party at any time.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@nothoi.com.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

We use Twilio to deliver SMS messages. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy. We do not share your information with any other third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@nothoi.com.
