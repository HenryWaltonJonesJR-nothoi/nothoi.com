SMS Policy

LifeOS is a private AI assistant operated by a single individual. SMS notifications are sent only to contacts who have been personally authorized by the account owner.

Opt-In

To receive messages, a recipient must be personally known to the account owner and have their phone number added to an authenticated contacts list. Recipients may also text START to +1 (313) 800-0822 to confirm opt-in.

Opt-Out

Text STOP to +1 (313) 800-0822 at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive no further messages.

Message Types

System status alerts
Daily briefings
Conversational AI responses
Inbox and task notifications

Volume

Expected volume is under 100 messages per month across all recipients. No marketing or solicitation.

Help

Text HELP to +1 (313) 800-0822 for assistance. Contact the account owner directly for support.
