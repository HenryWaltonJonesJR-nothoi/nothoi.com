SMS Messaging Policy

Program Description

Nothoi LLC operates a private notification and executive briefing service. Registered contacts receive automated SMS messages including system status alerts, daily briefings, task confirmations, scheduling updates, and operational notifications.

Who Can Subscribe

Nothoi LLC's SMS service is available to clients and contacts who have been invited to use our services.

How to Opt In

To subscribe to SMS notifications from Nothoi LLC, enter your mobile phone number in the form below, check the consent box, and submit the form. By opting in, you confirm that you agree to receive automated text messages from Nothoi LLC. You understand that:

  • Message frequency: up to 10 messages per week
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • Consent is not required as a condition of any purchase
  • You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message
  • You can request help by replying HELP to any message

Your mobile number will not be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes at any time.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message from us. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. You may re-subscribe at any time by replying START or by completing the opt-in form above.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at support@nothoi.com.

Message Types

  • Task and action confirmations

  • Service announcements
